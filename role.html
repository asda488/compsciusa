The President

The president is in charge of making sure that the USA functions properly, in multiple ways.


The President's roles

The president has a lot of roles and powers, as granted by Article II of the constitiution, and here are some of these.

  • Commander-in-chief of the US armed forces - in charge of all the army, navy, marines and air force.

  • The right to veto legislation passed by the Senate and House of Representatives.

  • Pardon and reprieve any person or authority, including themselves.

  • Convene/adjourn Congress.

  • Appoint certain officals like Supreme Court justices.



The President's job

As the person in charge of the USA, they are in charge of representing the USA in world
diplomacy and summits, like the G7. They are also in charge of passing important laws and bills. Finally,
the president helps with morale and makes public speeches as neccesary.
