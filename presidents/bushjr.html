George W. Bush

  • Date of presidency: January 20, 2001 - January 20, 2009 (two terms)

  • First Lady: Laura Bush

  • Party: Republican

  • Vice President: Dick Cheney

  • Other: Former governor of Texas, invaded Iraq to get rid of its supposed WMDs. Won election by 537 votes out of roughly 6 million.
